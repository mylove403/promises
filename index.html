My Promise to You

  • Main har achhe aur bure waqt mein aapke saath rahunga.
  • Main hamesha aapki baatein dhyan se sununga aur samajhne ki koshish karunga.
  • Main har pal ko aapke saath jeene aur usse yaadgaar banane ka wada karta hoon.
  • Main aapke sapno ka sammaan karunga aur unhe pura karne mein madad karunga.
  • Main aapse bepanah pyaar karunga, aaj aur hamesha.
  • Main puri koshish karunga ki aapko kabhi takleef na ho, na mere wajah se aur na kisi aur wajah se.
  • Main ye kabhi bhi nahi bhulunga ki aap mere liye kitni maine rakhti ho.
  • Main ye kabhi nahi bhulunga ki agar aapne mauka diya hai toh ye mera akhiri mauka hai.
